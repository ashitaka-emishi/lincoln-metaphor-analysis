## Key Novel Instances

### inst_00018: "The war came"
The most radical agency-removal in the Lincoln corpus. The war is not caused, chosen, declared, or sent — it arrives as a natural event (like weather, flood, dawn). No agent is named. This is not Lincoln's rhetorical humility; it is a deep metaphysical claim embedded in the sentence's grammar. The passive-intransitive construction removes all human causal agency. This is cluster_06 (Providence) operating through grammatical structure rather than explicit theological language.

**Koenigsberg significance**: The obligatory frame is at its most absolute here. If the war "came" without human causation, resistance to it is incoherent. The war could not have been avoided because no one initiated it.

### inst_00024: Blood-lash-sword economy
"Every drop of blood drawn with the lash shall be paid by another drawn with the sword." This is the only place in Lincoln's corpus where slavery's physical violence (the lash on enslaved bodies) is named as the direct causal antecedent of the war's violence. The economy is: enslaver's blood extraction → God's blood extraction from the nation. Enslaved people's suffering is the first term in a divine accounting equation.

**Structural function**: This instance is the hinge of the Second Inaugural. It is why the address can say "malice toward none" — if God is the accountant, Lincoln's side is not taking vengeance. The absence of personal malice is structurally enabled by the presence of divine bookkeeping.

**Absence note**: Enslaved people appear as victims of the lash — but only as the first line of an equation. They are inputs to the theodicy, not agents of their own history.

## Two-Arc Extended Metaphor Structure

**ext_004 — Organism survival arc** (inst_00014, 00015, 00020, 00025, 00026)
- Opens: nation as body with hostile insurgent agents (S3)
- Midpoint: body at war with itself / autoimmune logic (S12)
- Resolution: "bind up the nation's wounds" (S17) — the physician role
- Logic: war = acute autoimmune crisis → post-war = wound-binding / convalescence

**ext_005 — Theodicy arc** (inst_00018, 00019, 00021, 00022, 00024)
- Opens: "the war came" — war as providential event, not human decision (S7)
- Midpoint: God wills slavery's removal; gives the war to both sides (S14, S15)
- Resolution: blood-debt paid by divine accounting (S16)
- Logic: if God is causal agent and divine accountant, the war is neither arbitrary nor revengeable

## Koenigsberg Constructs

**Fantasy type**: theodicy — God's will replaces human agency as the explanatory framework. This is the mature form of the cluster_06 logic that begins in Gettysburg ("under God").

**Violence logic**: divine_judgment — war is punishment for the national sin of slavery. The mechanism is debt-payment, not organism-purification (confirming disease_and_purification absent).

**Obligatory frame**: most absolute in the corpus. "The war came" + divine will = war was not a human choice and thus cannot be an object of political negotiation.

**Guilt distribution**: cosmic — both North and South bear guilt; God judges both. This is the terminal point of the guilt_distribution arc across the five-phase corpus. Trajectory: external (Gettysburg) → distributed → cosmic (Second Inaugural).

**Projected entity**: `lincoln_non_agent` flagged on 5 of 13 instances. Lincoln presents himself as reader of divine will, not its author. This is the most concentrated non-agency in the corpus.

**Sacrificial economy**: explicit and named. God draws blood from the nation in exact proportion to blood drawn by the lash. The accounting is precise.

## Diachronic Position

Document is Phase 5 (1864–1865, terminal) in the five-phase arc. Represents:
- Completion of the guilt_distribution arc (external → distributed → **cosmic**)
- Completion of the cluster_06 arc (minor note in Gettysburg → dominant frame in Second Inaugural)
- cluster_03 (experiment/proposition) displaced entirely — no instances. The republic is no longer a proposition under test; it is a sinner under divine judgment.

**Key diachronic shift**: In Gettysburg (1863), the war is a logical proof procedure (cluster_03 dominant alongside cluster_04). In the Second Inaugural (1865), the war is divine punishment (cluster_06 dominant, cluster_03 absent). This shift — from epistemological to theological frame — is the major diachronic finding of the corpus.
